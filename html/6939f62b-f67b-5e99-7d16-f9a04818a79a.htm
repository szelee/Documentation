Remarks

BACKGROUND

This FEWS allows a customer with a smartcard (recorded in their CBE customer record), to login to their CBE customer account by touching their smartcard against a ‘post’. The FEA will capture the smartcard number and provide this as input to the CBE so that the CBE can recall their Customer Id as output.

This FEWS must also support customer login via photocard for purchase of season tickets greater than 7 days at a TVM.

ASSUMPTIONS

  1. This FEWS assumes that the smart ‘post’ physical unit connects to the FEA either TVM or Ticket office machine and captures the smartcard number.
  2. The TVM should allow the customer to login via photocard id when purchasing a season ticket >7 days. This assumes that the customer has already set up an account and has recorded their photocard id.

VALIDATION

The following validation checks are made by the CBE on the data provided by the FEA. Failure to meet these checks will result in appropriate error messages as shown in the appropriate API guide.

  1. All mandatory fields are present.
  2. Smartcard number is valid (see ITSO format details).
  3. Smartcard or photocard id exists in CBE customer records.
  4. Photocard id reference or smartcard number must be present (but not both).

Examples
Example CustomerCardLoginViaSmartcard Request
Copy
<soapenv:Envelope xmlns:soapenv="http://schemas.xmlsoap.org/soap/envelope/" xmlns:ns="http://vixtechnology.com/cbe/2012-05-29">
  <soapenv:Header>
    <ns:UserCredentials>
      <ns:UserName>TSawyer</ns:UserName>
      <ns:Password>password</ns:Password>
    </ns:UserCredentials>
  </soapenv:Header>
  <soapenv:Body>
    <ns:UnlockRefundRequest>
      <ns:PhotocardId>126</ns:PhotocardId>
    </ns:UnlockRefundRequest>
  </soapenv:Body>
</soapenv:Envelope>
