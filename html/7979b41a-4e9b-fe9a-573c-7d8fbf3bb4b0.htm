Remarks
This case completes the sale with the payment confirmation details for Point to Point, season tickets, railcard sales and Gift eVouchers. (Gift eVouchers are not mixed with other products in a sales transaction.) Purchase confirmation provides all the remaining data for fulfilment activities and settlement and moves the sales transaction status from Awaiting authorisation to Confirmed.
