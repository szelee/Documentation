ICbeOnboardServiceHeartbeat Method

This case allows the retail channels especially TVMs to verify the health of the CBE and subsequently use it to take the TVMs out of Service or bring them back into service. It will also apply to the web and mobile to indicate when the service is unavailable so that the FEA can display a suitable error message for the various failure cases.

Cbe health status.
Remarks

BACKGROUND

The FEWS must fetch the availability status of CBE Database, IPTIS and LSM(3th party).

The table below shows the various states which can occur and indicates when the TVM should shut down or display one of several error messages:

CbeDataStoreStatusJourneyPlanningApiStatusLsmApiStatusProposed TVM State
GOODGOODGOODACTIVE
UNAVAILABLEGOODUNAVAILABLESHUTDOWN
GOODUNAVAILABLEUNAVAILABLESHUTDOWN
UNAVAILABLEUNAVAILABLEUNAVAILABLESHUTDOWN

BUSINESS PROCESS CONTEXT

Not required.

PROCESS DIAGRAM

Non provided.

ASSUMPTIONS

  1. If CBE or any of the critical interfaces are down it is assumed that the FEA will manage the customer experience and reduce the service offered to those that can be supported.
  2. It is assumed that the FEA will either display a suitable error message or shut down dependent on the service unavailable (in the case that the CBE heartbeat service is not available or times out).
  3. It is assumed that the FEA will either display a suitable error message or shut down dependent on the heartbeat services unavailable response (in the case that the CBE Data Store, IPTIS or LSM or all are not available).

VALIDATION

The following validation checks are made by the CBE on the data provided by the FEA. Failure to meet these checks will result in appropriate error messages as shown in the appropriate API guide.

  1. Mandatory data items not found in Input.
